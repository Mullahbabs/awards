People's-First Enterprise Awards 2025

Celebrating Excellence in Business Innovation

The Calabar Enterprise Awards is the premier event recognizing outstanding achievements and innovations in the business community of Calabar and beyond.

This prestigious ceremony honors enterprises that have demonstrated exceptional performance, creativity, and impact across various sectors including technology, retail, hospitality, manufacturing, and sustainability.

Join us in celebrating the visionaries and trailblazers who are shaping the future of business in our region. Your vote helps recognize and reward the exceptional contributions of these outstanding enterprises.
