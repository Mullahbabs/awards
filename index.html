Empowering Dreams, Transforming Futures!

The People First Enterprise Awards is not just an event; it's a grand celebration of remarkable individuals and businesses that are making waves in our community! We are dedicated to honoring those who deliver exceptional value to Cross River State, bridging the unemployment gap through integrity and stewardship. Your unwavering dedication, innovation, and resilience have played a pivotal role in the growth and development of our vibrant business sector.

This year, we’re turning up the excitement by spotlighting the incredible efforts of small businesses across our three Senatorial Districts! Join us in recognizing their impactful contributions to job creation, product innovation, community engagement, and their commitment to shaping a thriving economic landscape that inspires countless entrepreneurs.

Our celebration this year promises to be a banger! Get ready for an unforgettable night filled with inspiration, networking, and recognition. Let’s come together to empower dreams and transform futures!
